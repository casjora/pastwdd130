Overview

Purpose

To provide examples of my professional capabilities to potential employers. The overall goal is to be noticed by professionals that will help me develop a better professional network. Primarily, people will find examples of my work and see if they might need something similar to it.

Audience

Potential employers interested in reporting creation and analysis The ideal users are those working in a high-analysis role, where data analysis is the focus. Anyone looking to make data-driven decisions might have some insights on how to achieve it by looking into this website.
